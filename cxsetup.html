CX Setup

about

Made in Python by MF366, CXSetup is a scripting language created to be used alongside ContenterX.

However, anyone can use it in their own projects, if they wish to.

A well documented language, with a whole development suite that consists of syntax highlighters and even a linter (coming soon).
